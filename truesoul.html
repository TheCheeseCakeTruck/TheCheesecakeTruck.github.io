Welcome To The Cheesecake Truck!

Welcome to The Cheesecake Truck, where we bring you the heart and soul of Southern cuisine on wheels! Our vibrant and inviting truck is adorned with colorful designs that transport you to a world of rich flavors and comforting dishes.

As you approach our truck, the mouth-watering aroma of our slow-cooked meats, savory sauces, and freshly baked desserts will have you eagerly anticipating your first bite. Our menu board showcases a delectable selection of classic soul food staples that are sure to satisfy your cravings.

Our friendly and outgoing staff are passionate about sharing the stories and traditions behind each dish, providing a warm and welcoming atmosphere that makes you feel right at home. Whether you're enjoying a quick lunch on the go or catering a special event, our soul food truck promises a memorable dining experience that will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished, both body and soul.
